about me


Hey, there! You can call me Kirsten or Kir - I go by either.

I approach life with curiosity and a commitment to lifelong learning. I am fascinated by the things that make me wonder and am driven to dig in and understand those things. My inquisitive and investigative nature led me to jump into the vast world of technology, specifically web development, where I hope to help people in our ever-evolving, technologically-interconnected world. I am inspired to find connections among seemingly unrelated topics to pinpoint common threads and engage others in critical thought and action through aesthetic and interactive design.

My professional background is in Kinesiology and have been coaching for 6 years. It's a passion of mine - I love helping and motivating others. My experience as a coach has given me skills to observe whole systems (aka my clients and athletes), analyze for efficiency and help chart a path forward to reach a desired outcome, while taking into account wildcard variables (aka life) and prioritizing the system's whole wellbeing. I see these skills in the wellness arena as transferrable to technology and web development.

I enjoy listening to music and podcasts, longboarding, water coloring, hiking in mountains and being in nature. I am passionate about mental health, accessible health care and building a just and equitable world.
